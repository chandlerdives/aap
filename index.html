    • POLYTHENE

    The staple of our business. When it comes to polythene there isn't much we don't know or can't supply! We've a huge range to offer including pallet covers, top sheets, single wound film, bags, liners and tubing. We supply all blends of films and offer an eco-friendly range too, so it's no surprise polythene still plays a crucial role in packaging and will for many years to come.

    CARDBOARD

    Boxes anyone? If it needs to go into a box, we can help. Working with some of the largest manufacturers in the UK, we can supply sheet board and cartons to suit all needs.

    Single Walled, Double Walled, Pallet Boxes, Octibins the list goes on! Boxes are here to stay and we can help find your perfect solution.

    PALLETWRAP STRETCHFILM

    Call it what you like, we know what you mean! The range for this product is huge. From the super thin 5mu pre-stretch to the heavy duty 34mu 'Daddy' of wraps, we've got them all covered! We'll find the product that not only suits your packing needs but also ticks the box when it comes to pricing and minimising polythene usage.

    AAP can be your 'one-stop shop' for everything packaging, but if for some unknown reason that we can't help we'll be sure to point you in the right direction of someone who can. After all that's what excellent customer service is, right?
