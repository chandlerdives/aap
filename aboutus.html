About Us.

We have over 20 years of experience in the packaging industry and have specialised knowledge in the polythene sector. We are building our business with a primary focus on our customer's needs and we truly believe that our service levels will not be beaten!

At AAP, we strive to exceed customer expectations and build long term partnerships. As agents for several of the UK's leading manufacturers, we ensure you are buying the right product, from the right supplier at the right price. Every customer benefits from a technical account manager who spends time understanding their requirements and ensuring outstanding customer service is always met. We service all sectors and focus on saving you time, money and becoming a more sustainable business. That's why we are All About Packaging!

Kevin Pitcher

Having gained extensive knowledge working in the packaging manufacturing industry for 20 years I decided it was time for a change and branched out on my own. In 2018 AAP was born and I have never looked back!

From my working experience I felt there was a gap in the market for a company to provide clients with an affordable 'One Stop Shop Solution' for all their packaging needs. Over the last 20 years I have built up a vast knowledge of packaging solutions to suit a wide array of industry sectors and believe AAP have proven to be the perfect fit for many of our clients. Furthermore, I wanted to develop a packaging waste and recycling service to help my clients promote environmental sustainability. I firmly believe that if we all make small changes they can make a big difference.

AAP's philosophy is to provide top customer service and help our clients find optimal and cost effective solutions for their packaging and recycling requirements. We want to work and develop long term partnerships with you - that's the bottom line!

Outside of work I enjoy spending time with my family, travelling to new places and seeing new things. I am a keen golfer currently playing off a handicap of 8 and enjoy all sports. I like coffee in the mornings, white with one sugar and tea in the afternoon, white with no sugar. My ideal accompaniment for both of these would be a Jammie Dodger, but just the one, I need to watch the waist line!

Lee Divers

1999, the year my packaging journey began! When I started working in the industry all those years ago I had no idea that one day I would be a partner in my own packaging business but here we are!

Those early years helped me understand the importance of really listening to customers and understanding their problems and needs. I've been fortunate to learn my trade by working alongside some great people within the industry and early on I recall one of them telling me to 'look after the customer and the customer will look after you'.
