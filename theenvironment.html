The Environment.

How do we help?

AAP are committed to minimising the impact on our planet of the packaging we supply. We can offer high strength alternatives that help to reduce packaging weight without compromising performance or quality. We can offer products made of recycled materials if suitable to your application. All of our packaging is 100% recyclable. Despite the bad press plastics have received, accurate research has shown that plastic packaging is a lightweight and resource efficient product saving packaging mass, energy and greenhouse gas emissions. Packaging items in plastics can help to significantly reduce waste and save energy. This makes plastic packaging the best choice for businesses and the planet. How we RECYCLE and DISPOSE of our packaging waste is the key to protecting our environment in the long term. With this in mind we have partnered with a packaging waste expert. We can help to improve your packaging waste streams to improve your green credentials and often save you money at the same time!
