The Environment.

How do we help?

At All About Packaging we care about our planet and the environmental impacts associated with plastic packaging. That's why we are committed to minismising the imapct on our planet of the packaging we supply.

We can offer high strength alternatives that help to reduce packaging weight without compromising performance or quality, as well as products made of recycled materials if suitable to your application.

All of our packaging is 100% recyclable. Despite the bad press plastics have received, accurate research has shown that plastic packaging is a lightweight and resource efficient product saving packaging mass, energy and greenhouse gas emissions. Packaging items in plastics can help to significantly reduce waste and save energy. This makes plastic packaging the best choice for businesses and the planet.

How we RECYCLE and DISPOSE of our packaging waste is the key to protecting our environment in the long term. With this in mind we have partnered with a packaging waste expert - We can help to improve your packaging waste streams to improve your green credentials and often save you money at the same time!

What more can we do?

In recent years there has been a huge shift in how customers handle and recycle the waste their business generates. For some time polythene and single use plastics have been considered 'dirty products', however we strongly believe that polythene and cardboard in particular are hugely important materials in packaging and will continue to be around for many years to come. So it makes sense to consider how well these so called dirty products are handled and disposed of, that's where we can help, guide and deliver.

We actively encourage all our clients and customers to consider how they effectively handle the waste generated on a daily basis. Working with our recycling partners we can supply a range of waste handling equipment to help customers:

  • save money on their weekly bin collections
  • generate income from their waste stream by using compactors and balers
  • save valuable work space & car parking by reducing the amount of bins required on site
  • reduce their carbon footprint due to fewer bin collections

    • We visit premises to complete surveys before offering a comprehensive quotation detail where savings can be made and how income can be generated. We can tailor packages to suit all business, including outright purchase, leasing or renting machines over 3 or 5 years. We believe working together we can make a small step towards becoming a cleaner, greener, happier place to live.
